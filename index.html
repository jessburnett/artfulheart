Welcome to The Artful Heart

The Artful Heart's mission is to enhance the quality of life for those with life, physical, cognitive, or emotional challenges through the therapeutic use of art. Our programs have been developed around the philosophy that creative expression is central to healing and wellness. In a safe, creative space, a person is invited, guided, and encouraged by the highly trained, artists and therapists to rediscover him or herself through the art making process.

"Every man must decide whether he will walk in the light of creative altruism or in the darkness of destructive selfishness."
